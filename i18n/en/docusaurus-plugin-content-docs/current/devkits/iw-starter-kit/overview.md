# iW-ST Starter Kit

The `iW-ST Starter Kit` is a compact devkit based on the **STM32F030F4P6**, designed for anyone who wants to explore the STM32 ecosystem with a focused board that is simple to power and ready for quick tests.

![iW-ST Starter Kit board](/img/iw-starter-kit/BOARD_VIEW.png)

---

## Highlights

- 32-bit Arm Cortex-M0 MCU
- Clock up to 48 MHz
- 16 KB of Flash and 4 KB of SRAM
- Programming over USB Type-C
- SPI, I2C, ADC, timers, PWM and RTC

---

## Main specifications

### Processing

- STM32F030F4P6
- Arm Cortex-M0 core
- Frequency up to 48 MHz

### Memory

- 16 KB of Flash
- 4 KB of SRAM with hardware parity

### Analog and timing

- 12-bit ADC with up to 9 channels
- Conversion range from 0 to 3.6 V
- 5 timers for measurement, PWM and capture
- RTC with alarm and periodic wakeup

### Communication

- SPI with programmable 4-to-16-bit frames
- I2C with Fast Mode Plus support

---

## Where this board makes sense

- Studying and validating STM32 peripherals
- Simple control prototypes
- Small local interfaces and automation
- Low-cost projects with an ARM MCU
