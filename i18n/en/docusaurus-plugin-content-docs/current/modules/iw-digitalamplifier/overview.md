# iW-DigitalAmplifier

The `iW-DigitalAmplifier` is a breakout board aimed at digital-audio projects that need good power, high efficiency and simple integration with embedded audio sources.

![iW-DigitalAmplifier board](/img/iw-digitalamplifier/BOARD_VIEW.png)

---

## Highlights

- `TAS5805` IC
- Class-D stereo amplifier
- up to 23 W per channel
- digital input via I2S or TDM
- low thermal dissipation
- compact form factor for prototypes

---

## Ideal applications

- wireless speakers
- smart speakers
- IoT devices with audio
- DIY digital-audio prototypes

---

## Why this module is useful

When the goal is to quickly validate a product with audio, the iW-DigitalAmplifier avoids spending time developing a power stage from scratch. It helps the team focus on the DSP, the user experience and the integration with the rest of the system.
