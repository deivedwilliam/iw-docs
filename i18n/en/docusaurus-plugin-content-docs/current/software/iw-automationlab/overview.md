# iW-AutomationLab

`iW-AutomationLab` is software aimed at simulating, analyzing and validating **MODBUS** communications. It was designed to make life easier for engineers, technicians and integrators who need to test both serial networks and Ethernet connections.

![iW-AutomationLab interface](/img/iw-automationlab/SOFTWARE_VIEW.webp)

---

## Highlights

- complete MODBUS simulation
- operation as Master or Slave
- support for RTU over RS485
- support for MODBUS TCP/IP
- reading and writing on real devices
- an interface designed to reduce operational error

---

## Usage modes

### Simulation

- creation of custom registers
- emulation of devices for bench testing
- validation of scenarios before the field

### Analysis and testing

- reading real equipment
- writing registers
- network and protocol troubleshooting

### Serial integration

- use with the `iW-IND-RS485` converter
- focused on industrial environments and automation

---

## Target audience

- automation engineers
- maintenance technicians
- system integrators
- commissioning and bench teams

---

## Value of the tool

iW-AutomationLab helps shorten test cycles, reduce trial and error, and bring more predictability to the development of MODBUS-based systems. For those working with RTU and TCP/IP in the same context, it also centralizes the routine into a single tool.
