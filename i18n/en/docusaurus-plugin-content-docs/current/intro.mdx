# Official documentation of Indústrias William

Welcome to the official documentation for the boards and solutions from **Indústrias William**.

This portal was organized to serve as a living technical reference. The idea is to bring together, in one place, product summaries, hardware details, firmware flows, usage examples and links to supporting materials.

---

## What you'll find here

- Overview of the boards and modules in the IW line
- Getting-started guides for hardware, firmware and integration
- Practical examples to speed up validation and prototyping
- Links to images, pinouts and technical materials
